Som Tum

Known as papaya salad.There are many variations of som tum, including Tam Pu Pla Ra and Tam Khorat.

Gaeng naw mai

Known as keng no mai or Lao bamboo soup, is a traditional soup.It's often served with sticky rice, which is a staple in Lao and northeastern Thai cuisine.

Sai krok Isaan

It's made with ground pork, pork fat, garlic, salt, and cooked sticky rice.It's commonly served with fresh ginger, chilies, and sometimes cabbage.

Tom Saep

It is a simple hot and sour Thai pork rib soup is a simple hot and sour Thai pork rib soup.It has many similarities to Tom Yum.The soup really showcases the fragrant depths of classic aromatic Thai herbs.

Grilled chicken

Known as gai yang.It is made by marinating a whole spatchcocked chicken with a paste of garlic.
